A locally-made tower is the backbone of Yaee's first cellular network. The town's network is composed of two antennas and an open-source base station from a Canadian company called <a href="http://nuranwireless.com/news/nuran-wireless-selected-rhizomatica-connect-mexico%E2%80%99s-rural-communities">NuRAN</a>.
